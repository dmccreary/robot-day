    RGB Box

    This little box is one of the favorite kits of our staff. It has a power switch and three potentiometers (pots) hooked up to a Raspberry Pi Pico. The pots control the brightness of the red, green and blue pixels on a low-cost LED strip of about 10 pixels. The total part cost is around $11.

    Parts List

    1. Raspberry Pi Pico - $4
    2. 10-element NeoPixel Strip (WS2811B) - $3
    3. Breadboard - $1.50
    4. 3 potentiometers - 50 cents each
    5. 3 AA battery pack - $1
